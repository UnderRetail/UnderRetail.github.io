Frequently Asked Questions

How long does each investment cycle take? +
Each investment cycle typically takes 3 weeks from capital investment to profit distribution.

The minimum investment is £40 for our Bronze tier. There is no maximum investment limit.
How are profits calculated and distributed? +
Profits are calculated based on the final sale price minus costs. Returns are distributed automatically to your registered payment method within 3 weeks.
Can I track my investments? +
Yes, all investors receive access to a personal dashboard where you can track your investments, returns, and upcoming payouts.
